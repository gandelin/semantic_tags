Top Backpacking Tents of 2016

Things to consider to find the best tent for backpacking

  • Season Rating

    • You need to consider what weather conditions you will be backpacking in, whether you will be going in three or four seasons.

  • Weight

    • Remember you will be carrying your pack for days. The lighter the tent the better. Aim for 3 lbs. per person.

  • Number of People

    • Backpacking tents usually range in size from 1 - 4 people. If you are a large person you may consider bumping up to the next larger size.

  • Number of Doors

    • Most packpacking tents have one or two doors. With two doors it is usually better for two reasons. First you don't have to climb over a fellow camper and second you have an additional vistibule in which to put your gear.
